Notificapp lets you take control of your Android notifications. Create rules that match notifications and trigger actions — dismiss, snooze, flash alerts, alarms, webhook delivery — and, unlike other notification automation apps, extract the data inside notifications into a structured, reusable dataset.

All rule matching, data extraction, and storage happen entirely on-device. No cloud sync, no analytics, no network access without your explicit configuration.

Common uses:

* Cut through notification noise — auto-dismiss or snooze what doesn't matter, get alerted for what does
* Turn bank and payment notifications into a spending dataset
* Turn carrier notifications into delivery status and tracking numbers
* Bridge extracted data to home automation systems like Home Assistant via webhooks

Notificapp is free and open source software (GPL-3.0). Rules can be exported, shared, and imported as plain JSON — the community rule library grows with every contribution.
